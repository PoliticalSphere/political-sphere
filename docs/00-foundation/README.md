Welcome to the foundation documentation for Political Sphere. This section establishes the core principles, vision, and fundamental concepts that guide our multiplayer political simulation game.

## Overview

The foundation documents provide the essential context and principles that underpin all aspects of Political Sphere. These documents are foundational to understanding our mission, values, and approach to creating an engaging and responsible political simulation platform.

## Purpose

These foundation documents serve several important purposes:

1. **Alignment**: Ensure all team members understand our core mission and values
2. **Decision Making**: Provide guidance for product and business decisions
3. **Consistency**: Maintain consistent messaging and approach across the organization
4. **Onboarding**: Help new team members quickly understand our context
5. **Strategy**: Support strategic planning and roadmap development

## Maintenance

Foundation documents should be reviewed and updated regularly to ensure they remain relevant and accurate. Major updates should be discussed and approved by leadership.
